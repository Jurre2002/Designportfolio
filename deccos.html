More information

This project focused on a platform that highlights the social impact companies have on the world. Our task was to redesign the platform to make it more understandable and user-friendly. To achieve this, we primarily applied Nielsen’s 10 usability heuristics.

We concentrated on improving the platform’s structure and flow, ensuring that users were gradually and clearly guided through the content. It was a challenging project, as the platform’s underlying purpose was initially difficult to grasp.
