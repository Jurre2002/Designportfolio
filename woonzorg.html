More information

The goal of this project was to encourage older adults to be more physically active. Our role was to design and add a new feature to an existing senior living app to support this goal.

The fun, yet challenging part of the project was finding the right types of activities that suited the needs and abilities of older adults. It was also very insightful to test these activities together with them and learn directly from their feedback.
